Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions / Bureau du surintendant des institutions financières

Other Topics

OSFI survey results

In the fall of 2018, Phoenix Strategic Perspectives, an independent research firm, conducted a confidential survey on OSFI’s behalf with executives of the financial institutions that OSFI regulates to explore perceptions of OSFI’s performance in the discharge of a number of key elements of its mandate. A summary of the results of the study are now available on OSFI’s website.

CPAB-OSFI roundtable

The Canadian Public Accountability Board (CPAB) and OSFI co-hosted a roundtable on audit quality in Canada in the fall. The event was an opportunity for more than forty of Canada’s public accounting and regulatory leaders to discuss ways to support continued financial system stability by enhancing public confidence in audit quality.

Margin requirements

OSFI published a letter in late June to provide guidance on the BCBS-IOSCO statement on margin requirements related to transitioning away from interbank offer rates and on-boarding of phase 4 and 5 counterparties for initial margin requirements.

Securities Administration requests

Effective December 9, 2019, the Regulatory Affairs Division in Ottawa will begin processing all Securities Administration Unit requests. This letter explains OSFI’s reasoning behind the change, efficiencies to be gained and what stakeholders can expect.

Ted Price

OSFI employees were saddened to learn that former Deputy Superintendent Edward (Ted) Price died on October 8. Ted joined OSFI from the private sector in 2001 and appointed Assistant Superintendent, Supervision in 2006. In 2011, he was named Deputy Superintendent. He retired from OSFI in July 2013. Ted's leadership through the 2008 financial crisis and its aftermath served OSFI and Canada well.
