Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions / Bureau du surintendant des institutions financières

Pensions

InfoPensions

In late November, OSFI issued InfoPensions - Issue 22 - November 2019, which includes announcements and reminders for federally regulated private pension plans and pooled registered pension plans. It also describes how OSFI applies selected provisions of the Pension Benefits Standards Act, 1985 (PBSA), Pooled Registered Pension Plans Act, their regulations, directives and OSFI guidance.

DB guide on reducing benefits

In May, OSFI issued proposed revisions to the Instruction Guide for Authorization of Amendments Reducing Benefits in Defined Benefit Pension Plans.
